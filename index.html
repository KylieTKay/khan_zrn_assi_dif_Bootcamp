PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

The world of new media is bursting with countless career possibilities that challenge your imagination and offer an impactful and enjoyable entertainment experience to the audience. If you aspire to a career in creating awe-inspiring content, the Interactive Media Design program can equip you with the essential skills to translate your creativity into digital media design, development, and production.

  • Web Design

    Web design is the process of planning, conceptualizing, and implementing the plan for designing a website in a way that is functional and offers a good user experience.

  • Programming

    Programming is the process of giving a set of instructions to a computer to make it able to perform a particular task. It involves designing, writing, testing, and maintaining computer programs.

  • Animation

    Animation is the process of creating the illusion of motion and change by rapidly displaying a sequence of static images that minimally differ from each other.

  • Video Editing

    Video editing is the process of manipulating and arranging video footage, audio elements, and visual effects to create a new piece of footage.
