PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

The world of new media is bursting with countless career possibilities that challenge your imagination and offer an impactful and enjoyable entertainment experience to the audience. If you aspire to a career in creating awe-inspiring content, the Interactive Media Design program can equip you with the essential skills to translate your creativity into digital media design, development, and production.

Web Design

Web design is the process of planning, conceptualizing, and implementing the plan for designing a website in a way that is functional and offers a good user experience.

Programming

Programming is the process of giving a set of instructions to a computer to make it able to perform a particular task. It involves designing, writing, testing, and maintaining computer programs.

Animation

Animation is the process of creating the illusion of motion and change by rapidly displaying a sequence of static images that minimally differ from each other

Video Editing

Video editing is the process of manipulating and arranging video footage, audio elements, and visual effects to create a new piece of footage.

What is Bipolar Disorder?

Bipolar disorder is a mental health condition that causes unusual shifts in mood, energy, and activity levels. It is also known as manic-depressive illness or manic depression

Our Client

The client is an organization that provides mental health services to individuals with bipolar disorder. They aim to improve the functional ability of their clients and help them manage their symptoms through therapy and medication.

The Interactive Media Design program aims to develop an interactive media platform that educates people about bipolar disorder. The platform will allow users to engage with the content and provide feedback, making them active participants in the learning process.

The IDP students developed a project that has been successful in educating people about bipolar disorder. The program has received positive feedback from users who have reported feeling more informed and supported. The program has also been recognized by mental health organizations for its effectiveness in educating people about bipolar disorder.
